About Easy Counter

Easy Counter is a versatile and user-friendly timer application designed to help you manage your time effectively. Whether you're cooking, working out, studying, or managing work sessions, our intuitive timer provides the tools you need to stay on track.

Our mission: To provide a simple yet powerful timing solution that helps people be more productive and mindful of their time.

Our Story

Easy Counter began in 2022 when our founder, a productivity enthusiast, became frustrated with the complicated timer applications available on the market. Most timers were either too basic with limited functionality or too complex with overwhelming features.

With a vision to create something that struck the perfect balance between simplicity and functionality, Easy Counter was born. Our team of developers and designers worked together to create an intuitive interface that anyone could use, while incorporating powerful features for those who needed them.

Key Features

  • Multiple Timer Types: Countdown, stopwatch, interval, and pomodoro timers in one application
  • Customizable Alerts: Choose from various alert sounds or upload your own
  • Visual Themes: Select from light, dark, blue, or green themes
  • Save & Share: Save your timer configurations and share them with others
  • Full Screen Mode: Perfect for presentations or when you need maximum visibility
  • Mobile Friendly: Works seamlessly across devices
